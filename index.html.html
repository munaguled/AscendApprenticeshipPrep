Most educational institutions focus on university applicants, coaching them on how to sell their academic abilities to universities. Why can't they do the same for apprentice applicants?

The reasons are twofold: a lack of specialized knowledge on the apprenticeship application process, and a disproportionate focus on the larger pool of university applicants.
